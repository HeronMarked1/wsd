Lesson Information

Guitar Lessons

The curriculum for guitar instruction is designed to provide the student with a well-rounded learning experience and will include the following:

  • Guitar technique
  • General music theory
  • Various songs, each chosen for its ability to advance the student's skill and enjoyment
  • Both electric and acoustic guitars will be covered

Music Theory Lessons

The curriculum for music theory instruction is designed to provide the student with a well-rounded learning experience, and will include the following:

  • Musical scales (major, minor, modes and blues)
  • Musical notation
  • Composition of triads and chords>
  • Intervals
  • And more!

Need to get more guitar strings, picks or other accessories? Head over to Guitar Center - they will have what you need!

About the Instructor

Gaston Kurtz

Gaston is an instructor in guitar, music theory, and bass guitar. He has twelve years of experience with the guitar, a Bachelor of Arts with a minor in Music Theory, and a total of 57 undergraduate credit hours in music. He is currently a member of the Christian worship band "Falling Forward," is the student pastor at Gateway Baptist Church in Spring, Texas and has been involved with music ministry for nine years. He loves teaching, and seeks to bring his students to a high level of proficiency.
